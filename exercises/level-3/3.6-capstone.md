# Exercise 3.6: Capstone -- The Transformation Plan

## Scenario Context

You've reached Week 12. The rollout is wrapping up, and Lena wants a comprehensive transformation plan document -- something that captures everything you've built so that it can live beyond your direct involvement.

> "I want a document I can hand to any new VP of Product, any new PM, or any board member who asks 'how does your product team work?' It should be the definitive reference for how Relay does spec-driven development."

This is your capstone: a complete, polished transformation plan that brings together your executive brief, tooling strategy, adoption plan, metrics dashboard, and sustainability plan into a single coherent document.

---

## Template Exercise

**Deliverable:** A comprehensive transformation plan using `transformation-plan-template.md`, plus a self-assessment using the Level 3 capstone rubric.

**Template:** [specs/templates/transformation-plan-template.md](../../specs/templates/transformation-plan-template.md)

### Steps

1. **Copy the template** to `my-work/level-3/spec-driven-transformation-plan.md`.

2. **Write the Executive Summary.** Synthesize the entire 90-day journey in 3-5 sentences: what you changed, why, what results you got, and what's in place for sustainability. This paragraph should stand alone.

3. **Write the Tooling Strategy section.** Pull from your Exercise 3.2 work but polish and update it. Reflect what actually happened (which tools did teams adopt? what worked? what didn't?).

4. **Write the Team Onboarding Plan section.** Pull from Exercise 3.3 but include what you learned. Which coaching strategies worked? Which teams surprised you? What would you do differently if you rolled out to a 5th team?

5. **Write the Adoption Metrics Dashboard section.** Pull from Exercise 3.4, updated with final Week 12 data. Project where each metric will be at Week 24 and Week 52 based on current trends.

   For Week 12, use these data points (filling in the scenario):
   - Spec completion rate: Core Platform 100%, Integrations 80%, Mobile 60% (micro-specs complicate counting), Enterprise 100%
   - Cycle time: down 15% on average across all teams (Core Platform already had the benefit; other teams are earlier in their curve)
   - Post-launch rework: down 35% for Integrations (biggest improvement), flat for Mobile, down 20% for Enterprise
   - Engineering confidence at kickoff: up from 2.8 to 3.9 average across all teams

6. **Write the Sustainability Plan section.** Pull from Exercise 3.5, refined into a crisp plan with clear owners, timelines, and success gates.

7. **Write the Budget and Resources section.** Calculate the total investment (PM hours, your hours, tooling costs) and the estimated ROI. Use real numbers from the scenario data.

8. **Write the Timeline section.** Create a retrospective timeline showing what actually happened in each phase, not just what was planned. Note where the plan diverged from reality and why.

9. **Final review pass.** Read the entire document end to end. Check for:
   - Internal consistency (do the metrics in the dashboard match the claims in the executive summary?)
   - Completeness (is there anything a new reader would need to understand that isn't here?)
   - Conciseness (is every section earning its place?)
   - Actionability (can someone pick this up and continue the work without you?)

10. **Score yourself** using the [Level 3 Capstone Rubric](../../rubrics/level-3-capstone-rubric.md). For each criterion, rate yourself 1-5 with justification. Save in `my-work/level-3/capstone-self-assessment.md`.

11. **Final commit:**
    ```
    git add my-work/level-3/
    git commit -m "Level 3 Capstone: complete transformation plan + self-assessment"
    ```

### What "Done" Looks Like

Your completed `my-work/level-3/` folder should contain:
- `spec-driven-executive-brief.md` -- the executive brief
- `tooling-strategy.md` -- the tooling strategy
- `team-adoption-plan.md` -- team-by-team adoption plans
- `adoption-metrics-dashboard.md` -- metrics and Week 6 analysis
- `sustainability-plan.md` -- long-term sustainability
- `spec-driven-transformation-plan.md` -- the comprehensive plan (capstone)
- `capstone-self-assessment.md` -- rubric-based self-assessment
