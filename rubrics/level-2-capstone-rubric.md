Use this rubric to self-assess your complete spec-driven development cycle for Smart Dependencies. Score each criterion 1-5 and write a brief justification.

---

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Significant gaps. This area needs substantial rework. |

## Criteria

### 1. Plan Quality

Does the sprint plan break the spec into a logical sequence of work items with realistic estimates, clear dependencies, and appropriate risk management?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Plan is a flat list of tasks with no sequencing or dependency logic. Resource constraints aren't addressed. No risk identification. An engineering team couldn't use this to plan their sprints. |
| 3 | Plan breaks requirements into work items, assigns owners, and sequences them across sprints. Resource constraints (David's 50% capacity) are acknowledged. 2-3 risks identified. Each sprint has a general goal, though shippable increments may not be well-defined. |
| 5 | Plan has clear work item decomposition with realistic effort estimates. Dependencies between work items are mapped and drive the sequence. Resource constraints shape the plan (not just noted as risks). Each sprint produces a demonstrable, shippable increment. Risks have specific mitigations, not just awareness. Traceability from spec requirements to work items is complete. |

### 2. Handoff Clarity

Does the handoff document give the engineering team what they need to build with confidence, beyond what the spec already provides?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Handoff is a copy/paste of the spec or a generic "here's what we're building" summary. Doesn't add value beyond the spec itself. Kickoff agenda is a monologue format. |
| 3 | Handoff highlights key decisions with rationale, identifies 3+ "watch out for" items, and lists open questions for Sprint 1. Kickoff agenda includes time for team questions. An engineer would feel oriented after reading this. |
| 5 | Handoff is a distinct, valuable document: key decisions are explained with the "why" behind non-obvious choices, edge cases are flagged with specific guidance, and open questions have owners and deadlines. Kickoff agenda is designed for interaction (not presentation). The document bridges product intent and engineering execution -- it translates, not just transmits. |

### 3. Iteration Efficiency

When mid-cycle problems arose, did you make clear decisions quickly while preserving the feature's core value?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Decisions were deferred, unclear, or contradictory. The spec amendment doesn't exist or doesn't reflect what actually changed. The revised plan doesn't account for the changes. |
| 3 | Each problem was triaged with options and a clear recommendation. Decisions preserved the core value proposition. Spec amendment documents what changed. The revised plan is updated but may have gaps in how changes ripple through the timeline. |
| 5 | Problems are triaged with structured analysis (blocker vs. scope change vs. enhancement). Each decision includes 2+ options with clear rationale for the chosen path. Spec amendment is traceable: what changed, why, and the impact on success criteria and timeline. The revised plan fully incorporates changes. Decisions demonstrate strong product judgment -- protecting what matters, cutting what doesn't. |

### 5. Outcome Assessment Rigor

Does the outcome assessment provide an honest, data-driven evaluation of what shipped versus what was specced, with actionable lessons?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Assessment is superficial ("it went well") or defensive. No connection between success criteria and actual results. Lessons are generic ("write better specs next time"). |
| 3 | Spec fidelity is scored with honest rationale. Success criteria are compared to actuals with clear "Met / Partially Met / Not Met" judgments. 2-3 specific lessons for next time. Qualitative feedback is summarized. |
| 5 | Assessment is rigorous and self-aware. Spec fidelity score is justified with specific examples of what stayed and what changed. Success criteria comparison includes honest analysis of why targets were or weren't met. "What to Write Differently" lessons are specific enough to change behavior (not just attitudes). Qualitative feedback is synthesized into patterns. Follow-up actions have owners and timelines. |
