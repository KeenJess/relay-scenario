### 5. Sustainability Realism

Will this practice survive 6 months after your direct involvement ends?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Plan depends entirely on the transformation lead's ongoing involvement. No embedding in existing rituals. No champions program. No plan for new team members. The practice would collapse within a month of you stepping away. |
| 3 | Specs are embedded into 3+ existing rituals. A champions program is defined. New PM onboarding flow exists. Warning signs are addressed with specific interventions. Exit criteria for the transformation lead are defined. |

Quarterly spec health review creates a self-correcting feedback loop. Exit criteria are specific and measurable. Failure modes are identified with early warning signs and mitigations.
